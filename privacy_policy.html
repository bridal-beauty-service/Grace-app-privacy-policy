Grace Beauty Studio - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Grace Beauty Studio. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Access

Our app requests access to your device's camera for the following purposes:

  • To capture photos for makeup tutorials and before/after comparisons
  • To enable virtual makeup try-on features
  • To allow users to save makeup looks and share them

2.2 Storage Access

We may access your device storage to:

  • Save captured photos and makeup looks
  • Load images from your gallery for makeup application
  • Cache app data for better performance

2.3 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Basic profile information
  • Makeup preferences and saved looks

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our makeup services
  • Personalize your experience with tailored makeup recommendations
  • Save your makeup preferences and created looks
  • Communicate with you about app updates and features
  • Improve our services through analytics

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Encrypted storage for sensitive information
  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Camera and Image Usage

Images captured through our app:

  • Are stored locally on your device by default
  • May be uploaded to our secure servers for backup if you choose to sync
  • Are not used for training AI models without your explicit consent
  • Can be deleted at any time from within the app

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Request a copy of your data

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@gracebeautystudio.com
  • App: Through the in-app contact form

10. Consent

By using Grace Beauty Studio, you consent to this Privacy Policy and the collection, use, and protection of your information as described herein.
